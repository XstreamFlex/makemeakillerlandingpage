Zero-Tracking Principles

1. Information We Collect

Xstream Flex is engineered to respect your operational boundary limits. We compile and push structural layout code directly to GitHub. In this capacity, we only collect data that is essential to authorize and run your platform tasks.

Account Registration Details: When you subscribe to the Founder's LTD or dynamic hosting upgrades, we store your email address, business profile names, transaction identifiers, and build status parameters.

Transactional Data: We do not gather or warehouse standard credit card details. All financial transactions are handled securely by PCI-DSS compliant third-party payment gateways (such as Stripe or Lemon Squeezy).

2. How We Process Data

Xstream Flex relies on automated pipeline procedures. We use your stored parameter configurations to complete the following tasks:

  • Convert structural inputs into formatted static files (HTML, CSS, JSON layouts).
  • Authenticate with GitHub repositories using OAuth credentials to push updates directly to your site pages.
  • Route automated notifications to your designated email regarding deployment achievements, errors, or security advisories.
  • Provide functional operational dashboards, billing portals, and live chat assistance.

3. GitHub API & OAuth Scope

To ensure absolute security over your source code ecosystem, our platform handles GitHub permissions with the highest levels of isolation:

  • Scoped Permissions: We request OAuth authorizations specifically bounded to the creation, read, and write operations of your specific public and private repositories containing Xstream Flex resources.
  • Isolation Boundaries: Our microservices cannot, and will never, browse, inspect, or store repositories, proprietary structures, or external software programs unrelated to Xstream Flex layouts.
  • Local Authority: You are the master of your GitHub account. You can revoke token connectivity instantaneously from your GitHub security console at any time without our intervention.

4. Code & Content Protection

Transient Compile Pipeline.

Xstream Flex is not a continuous hosting warehouse. Once our automated node compilers successfully establish structural frameworks and execute the direct commit-push events to your GitHub repository, your source files reside strictly on your personal spaces.

We retain no background copies of your proprietary configurations or customized static code layouts on our servers after deployment operations conclude. You retain 100% intellectual control, code ownership, and personal sovereignty over your properties.

5. Third-Party Disclosures

We strictly hold a zero-monetization stance on your details. We do not sell, barter, or distribute your email addresses, deployment telemetry, or system information to advertising conglomerates, brokerages, or marketing agencies.

Your information is shared with auxiliary subprocessors exclusively to complete your deployment requests:

⚙️ GitHub APIs (Direct Deployments)
⚙️ Cloudflare (Proxy and Edge Routing)
⚙️ Stripe (Secure Transaction Logs)
⚙️ Postmark (Deployment Notifications)

6. Security Infrastructure

We employ industry-standard protocols to shield records from exposure or alteration:

  • All network integrations leverage secure TLS 1.3 encryption mechanisms in transit.
  • OAuth tokens are fully encrypted at rest using AES-256 protocols with secure rotation sequences.
  • We implement strict IP-based system rules and automated rate limit systems on our API endpoints to resist brute-force operations.
  • Periodic vulnerability scanning runs across our build nodes to isolate and resolve system defects instantly.

7. Retention & Right to Erasure

We preserve standard transaction metadata parameters to satisfy global legal guidelines and tax compliance standards. Other operational profile items remain active as long as your LTD setup or subscription services exist.

If you wish to execute a permanent deletion (erasure) request, simply dispatch an email to our support team. We will remove your profile details, tokens, and build metadata from our databases within 72 business hours.

8. GDPR, CCPA, & Global Rights

Regardless of your physical location, Xstream Flex respects your fundamental rights regarding personal data:

  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can restrict our processing capabilities by terminating OAuth parameters inside your developer tools panel.
  • Right to Portability: Since your built site files live in your GitHub account, your content portfolios remain completely transferable at any time.
  • Non-Discrimination: We do not discriminate or charge premium rates if you choose to exercise your CCPA or GDPR data protection choices.

9. Cookies & Telemetry Limits

Xstream Flex strictly avoids intrusive tracking parameters:

We only deploy functional, structural cookies required to process login events, retain active dark mode settings, and verify compliance preferences. We do not integrate behavior trackers or tracking pixels to target advertisements around external web spaces.

10. Updates & Privacy Contact

We reserve the right to revise this Privacy Policy to ensure alignment with fresh build tools, hosting solutions, and emerging security regulations. If we make structural modifications, we will issue notifications via our core system dashboards or transactional emails.

For any questions, data access requests, or system integration feedback, please write directly to our privacy officer at privacy@xstreamflex.com.
